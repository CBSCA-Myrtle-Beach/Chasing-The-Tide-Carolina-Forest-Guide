The Community

Where Families Put
Down Roots

Nestled between the sandy shores of Myrtle Beach and the historic town of Conway, Carolina Forest is one of the fastest-growing master-planned communities along the Grand Strand. What started as International Paper timberland in the 1990s has grown into a thriving community of over 30 distinct neighborhoods — each with its own personality, but all sharing the same family-first feel.


Nearly half of the total land has been preserved as green space, giving Carolina Forest a canopy-covered character that sets it apart from the typical beach town. Tree-lined streets, interconnected walking trails, and neighborhood parks create an everyday quality of life that's hard to beat — and you're still just a short drive from the sand.


With easy access to Highways 501, 31, and International Drive, daily errands, the airport, and the beach are all within reach. It's the kind of place people move to and never want to leave.

Luxury · Gated

Waterbridge

A premier gated community surrounded by 120 acres of protected woodlands. Coastal cottage-style homes, 80+ acres of lakes, and SC's largest residential swimming pool headline the resort-caliber amenities.

Plantation Lakes

Custom-built luxury homes wrapped around 170 acres of lakes and ponds. Residents boat through 15 miles of stocked shoreline and enjoy two pools, tennis, basketball, and a sprawling amenities center.

An elegant gated community directly on the Intracoastal Waterway. Private boat landing, on-site boat storage, and direct ICW lots make this one of Carolina Forest's most coveted waterfront addresses.

Lifestyle

More Than Just a
Place to Live

Carolina Forest blends suburban convenience with coastal energy. Here's what everyday life actually looks like.
🛒

Shopping & Errands

Tanger Outlets on Hwy 501, plus a growing mix of grocery stores, pharmacies, and everyday services along Carolina Forest Blvd and International Drive. You rarely need to leave the area.

🍽️

Dining

The restaurant scene is growing fast — from craft burgers and sushi to tacos and Southern staples. New spots open regularly along Carolina Forest Boulevard, and you're still a short drive from the Grand Strand's full dining scene.

🌲

Outdoors & Recreation

The Hulk mountain bike and run park, Lewis Ocean Bay Heritage Preserve, walking trails through neighborhoods, and community pools and recreation centers. Active living is built into the fabric here.

⛳

Golf

Legends Golf Resort, Myrtle Beach National, and World Tour Golf Links are all minutes away. Carolina Forest puts you in the heart of the Grand Strand's golf corridor without the tourist traffic.

🏖️

Beach Access

Just 10–15 minutes to the sand via Hwy 31 or International Drive. Close enough to enjoy on a weekday evening, far enough inland to avoid the congestion and price tags of beachfront living.

🚗

Connectivity

Easy access to Hwy 501, SC-31, and International Drive. The Myrtle Beach airport is 15 minutes away. Whether you commute along the Grand Strand or work remotely, the location just works.
